🌉 Stop #2 – Hold onto your hats — we’re crossing into adventure! 🌬️ The West 7th Street Bridge isn’t just a way across the Trinity River — it’s a modern marvel. With its sleek, glowing arches and unique cable-stayed design, this 2013 structure redefined the Fort Worth skyline.

Connecting downtown to the bustling West 7th district, the bridge is your gateway to food, music, and mayhem. It’s where concrete meets cool. Snap a selfie, soak in the views, and let the breeze carry you into the night. This zone is all about motion, momentum, and crossing into something new. 🎶🌉🍸
