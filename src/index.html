Drink Tour Spring 2025

Welcome!
You and your party are invited to join a walking tour through downtown Fort Worth!
We'll start at the Linwood - Jesse D. Sandoval Park and visit five locations — all on foot. 🥾🌆

At our starting location we'll kick things off with a tasting of all available drinks
Each attendee will then draft 5 drinks into their inventory.
At each stop on the tour, we'll enjoy one of those drinks together. 📍🍻

The purpose of this event? To explore new spots and try new drinks with friends. 😁
There will be a mix of:

  • 🍸 Alcoholic drinks
  • 🍃 THC drinks
  • 🚰 Water and other non-alcoholic options

📅 Where and When?

Saturday May 10th, 5:45pm-11pm
301 Wimberly St, Fort Worth, TX 76107

Tasting starts at 6:15pm ⏰

🎒 What should I bring?

  • 👠 Walking shoes
  • 🧳 Bag to carry your drinks
  • 🍻 Drink assignment from host - NO GLASS - preferably chilled

Adventurous pups are welcome. Bring water for your dog as it is a long walk! 🐶💧

The route is 5.3 miles and should take 2 hours of walking to complete.
